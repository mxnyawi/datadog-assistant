## 🐶 Datadog Assistant , menu bar app for macOS

Datadog alerts you can't miss, right in your Mac menu bar. Free and open source.
Your keys stay on your Mac.

### Install (no Terminal)

1. Download **`Datadog-Assistant-Installer.zip`** below and open it , it unzips to
   **Datadog Assistant Installer**.
2. **Right-click** the app and choose **Open** (first time only, to get past the
   macOS unsigned-app prompt). If macOS still won't open it, go to
   **System Settings → Privacy & Security**, scroll down, and click **Open Anyway**.
3. Follow the steps: pick your Datadog site, sign in, and you're done. The 🐶
   appears in your menu bar.

Prefer the command line? See the [README](https://github.com/mxnyawi/datadog-assistant#-install-on-your-mac).
